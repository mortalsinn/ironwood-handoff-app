How to Use

Production Wizard Guide

1. Drafting the Blueprint

Start on the left side by selecting your project constraints (e.g., Wood Railing, Metal Spindles). As you check boxes, the right side automatically generates the required production stages and checklists tailored exactly to those materials.

2. Rearranging & Customizing

Drag & Drop: Every task on the right side has a drag handle on the left edge. Click and drag tasks to reorder them within a block.

Custom Items: Need something unique? Use the input boxes on the left panel (e.g., "Add Custom Fabrication") to inject bespoke, one-off tasks into the production run.

3. Smart @Mentions in Notes

When typing in the Production Notes box, you can type the @ symbol followed by a team member's name. A dropdown will appear allowing you to select them. When you push to CRM, the system automatically translates this into Zoho's hidden API format, triggering a real email notification to that user!

4. Miro / Mermaid Export

Click the purple "Copy Miro Code" button at the top to silently copy your entire production sequence to your clipboard. Paste this directly into a Miro board (or Mermaid JS viewer) and it will instantly generate a perfectly formatted, color-coded flowchart!

5. Pro Tips

  • Resizing: You can drag the middle divider between the left and right panels to give yourself more reading room! You can also drag the bottom-right corner of the outer popup to make the whole app bigger.
  • Auto-Save: The app silently saves your draft every time you type. If you accidentally close out, just reopen it on the same deal and everything snaps back!

How to use the Handoff App

1. Building the Blueprint

Use the Left Panel to select the structural components of the job. The Right Panel dynamically updates to show the required production stages and tasks. The app automatically determines what needs to happen based on what you check off.

2. Assigning Teams

By default, tasks are grouped into blocks (e.g., Railing Production & Install). You can assign an entire block to a user by clicking the Assign User dropdown. If you need to override a specific task within that block, click the team icon on that specific task.

3. Customizing the Workflow

  • Reordering: You can drag and drop any task on the Right Panel to reorganize the flow or move tasks between blocks.
  • Editing & Deleting: Click directly on any task name to edit the text inline. You can also delete any standard or custom task by clicking the × button next to it.
  • Adding Tasks: Use the "Click to add custom task..." dropdown inside any block to type a custom task.
  • Adding Blocks: Use the "Click to add custom item..." dropdowns on the Left Panel to generate entirely new blocks on the Right Panel!

4. Production Notes & @Mentions

At the bottom of the Left Panel, you can write Production Notes. Type @ followed by a team member's name to tag them. The system will convert this into a CRM Mention so they get a notification. You can click Add Another Note to separate different thoughts or messages into distinct CRM notes!

5. Auto-Save & Resizing

  • Resizing: You can drag the vertical divider between the left and right panels to adjust the width. You can also drag the bottom-right corner of the Right Panel to resize the entire app window!
  • Auto-Save: The app actively saves your entire layout—including custom tasks and drag-and-drop locations—every few seconds. If you accidentally close the window, just re-open it and your exact setup will be restored!

6. Finalizing & Pushing to CRM

When the job is completely verified and all tasks and assignments are correct, click the Mark Closed Won button in the top left header. This will officially update the Deal in Zoho CRM and push all your configured tasks to the assigned team members!
